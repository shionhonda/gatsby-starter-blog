## Mann–Whitney U Test 

$$
\begin{aligned}
U_1 = n_1n_2 + \frac{n_1(n_1+1)}{2} - R_1 \\
U_2 = n_1n_2 + \frac{n_2(n_2+1)}{2} - R_2 \\
U = \min{(U_1, U_2)}
\end{aligned}
$$
